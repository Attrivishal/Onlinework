About Forever Living Products

Our Heritage & Visionary Leadership

Forever Living Products began its journey in 1978 in Phoenix, Arizona, USA. Our founder, Rex Maughan, envisioned a company that would offer solutions for both better health and true financial freedom. His unwavering dedication has since transformed countless lives globally.

Our Inspiring Mission:

"We will create a profitable environment where individuals can, with dignity, be what they want to be. Where integrity, empathy and fun are our guides. We will create and cherish a passion for, and belief in, our company, our products and our industry. We will seek knowledge, and balance and above all, we will be courageous as we lead our company and its Forever Business Owners."
- Rex Maughan, Chairman and CEO

Global Footprint & Financial Strength

  • A robust, privately owned company with over $1.5 billion in assets – cash rich and debt free.
  • Operating successfully in over 160 countries, demonstrating consistent growth for over 40 years.
  • A proud community of more than 10 million Forever Business Owners (FBOs) worldwide.

Vertical Integration: Quality "From Plant to Product to You"

We maintain unparalleled quality and cost control by owning and managing every stage: from cultivating our vast aloe plantations (over 7500 acres with 50 million plants producing 65 million pounds annually) to manufacturing, R&D, quality control, and global distribution.

Advanced Manufacturing & Uncompromising Quality

  • Utilizing aseptic packaging for pure, preservative-free Aloe Drinks.
  • Our patented Stabilization Process flash heats the inner leaf gel, preserving freshness and boosting vitamin retention by 300%.
  • Forever Nutraceutical (FN) conducts 1.5 million quality tests annually, holding the prestigious ISO 17025 certification.
  • Aloe Vera of America (AVA) efficiently ships vast quantities of pure aloe products to over 160 countries.

Our Commitment to Environmental Stewardship

  • Recipient of the esteemed Blue Thumb Award for three consecutive years.
  • Achieved a 90% reduction in water usage through solar-powered pumps and advanced drip irrigation.
  • Our 50 million aloe plants actively cleanse the earth of hundreds of millions of tons of CO2 each year.
  • Proudly using shipping boxes made from 85% recycled materials and environmentally friendly inks.

Discover Our Superior Quality Products

At the heart of Forever's extensive product line is our pure, stabilized Aloe Vera. We are the world's leading grower and manufacturer of aloe vera and aloe vera based products, ensuring uncompromised quality from plant to product.

The Incredible Power of Aloe Vera

  • Optimal Variety: We cultivate `Aloe Barbadensis Miller`, known for its exceptional nutritional value.
  • Hand-Harvested Purity: Our aloe is meticulously hand-harvested, ensuring the purest inner leaf gel.
  • Nutrient Powerhouse: Packed with over 75 nutrients, including 20 minerals, 12 vitamins, and 18 amino acids.
  • Triple Action: Works in three stages: Cleansing (Lignin, Saponin), Nourishment (Vitamins, Minerals, Amino Acids), and Therapeutic (Enzymes, Anthraquinones).
  • Versatile Use: Ideal for both internal consumption and external skin application, mirroring skin's natural pH.

Why Choose Forever Aloe Vera Gel?

  • Pure Inner Gel: Contains only the highly nutritious inner gel, 100% safe for internal use.
  • Patented Stabilization: Our process locks in freshness and nutrients, unlike others.
  • All Natural: Free from artificial colors, flavors, homogenization, or excessive filtration.
  • Certified Excellence:Proudly certified by the IASC for superior potency and purity (99.7% pure).
